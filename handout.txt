A. Transmitter Specifications

   The three mystery signals are generated with the M6 transmitter
   standard in m6params.m, described in SRD, Chap 15, with the 
   following RF parameters:

   mysteryA:  SRRCLength:     5
              SRRCrolloff:    0.25
              T_t:            9e-6 s
              f_if:           1.88 MHz
              f_s:            820 kHz 

   mysteryB:  SRRCLength:     3
              SRRCrolloff:    0.35
              T_t:            7.3e-6 s
              f_if:           1.92 MHz
              f_s:            680 kHz

   mysteryC:  SRRCLength:     4
              SRRCrolloff:    0.3
              T_t:            8.2e-6 s
              f_if:           2.4 MHz
              f_s:            760 kHz
